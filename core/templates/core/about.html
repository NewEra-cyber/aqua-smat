Who We Are

Aquasmat is a team of passionate aquaculture experts, technologists, and entrepreneurs committed to revolutionizing fish farming in Africa.

Our diverse team brings together decades of experience in agriculture, finance, and technology to create sustainable solutions for smallholder farmers.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable, tech-driven aquaculture industry where smallholder farmers thrive as commercial entrepreneurs, eradicating poverty and hunger through innovative solutions.

Our Mission

We empower fish farmers with collateral-free financing, premium inputs, guaranteed markets, and cutting-edge technology through our integrated aquaculture platform.
