We are cherished by Fish Farmers

Martin Otieno

Fish Farmer, Kisumu

Honestly, before Aquasmat, I was just guessing. My yields were unpredictable, and I often worried about where I'd sell my fish. But the training they gave me was a game-changer. I learned so much about proper pond management and feeding. Plus, knowing I have a guaranteed market for my harvest means I can focus on growing quality fish without the stress. My family's finances are so much more stable now.

Daudi Osoo

Fish Farmer, Siaya

I was skeptical at first, but Aquasmat really delivered. The quality of their fingerlings and feed is unmatched, and it shows in the health and size of my fish. What really surprised me was the personalized support – they actually came to my farm and helped me troubleshoot issues. My profits have seen a significant jump, and I'm even planning to invest in a new pond next season. It's truly changed my outlook on fish farming.

Sarah Adhiambo

Mixed Farmer, Homa Bay

As a woman in fish farming, I sometimes felt isolated and lacked access to good resources. Aquasmat changed that for me. Their inclusive training programs and readily available, affordable feed made a huge difference. I used to struggle with disease outbreaks, but with their guidance, my fish are healthier than ever. Now, I'm not just a fish farmer; I'm a successful businesswoman contributing significantly to my household income. It's incredibly empowering.
