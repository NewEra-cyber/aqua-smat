About BDS Program

The Business Development Services program (BDS), is a fully sponsored program by Aquasmat that aims at enhancing the aquaculture SMEs to gradually develop from startups, grow and finally expand. This program also helps the SMEs to be able to acquire or access financial support in form of loans from the financial institutions since they are trained on importance of business compliance and financial management. Through this program most SMEs have been able to experience positive growth in terms of sales and profits. Aquasmat looks forward to engage more partners to enable the program reach at least 1000 SMEs in the aquaculture sector.

Our project transforms perceptions of aquaculture's potential for entrepreneurship and employment, building knowledge and capacity so young people, especially women, can access the sector's growth opportunities.

Program Details

BDS is designed to create sustainable opportunities for young people in aquaculture

Location Coverage

Operating in Kisumu, Homa Bay, Siaya, Kakamega, Busia, Migori counties

Eligibility

Open to youth aged 23-32 years from the six target counties

Our Goal

Create 150,000 sustainable jobs in aquaculture for young people
